Outcome: Ryanair

The claim was more or less made up and the Advertising Standards Authority promptly banned the ads. The biggest aviation emitter in Europe, Ryanair, has been sanctioned by the UK Advertising Standards Authority for misleading claims that it’s a green airline. The low-cost operator mislead consumers in press, TV and radio ads. Green campaign and research group Transport & Environment (T&E) said the ruling dispels Ryanair’s green myths and shows the need for lawmakers to finally do something about airline emissions. (transportenvironment.org, campaignlive.co.uk)
