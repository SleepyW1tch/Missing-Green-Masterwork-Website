Outcome: Nestlé

Faced criticism from environmental groups; no legal penalties reported. Nestlé was the worst polluter identified as part of an 8-day cleanup and brand audit of plastic pollution found at Freedom Island in the Philippines in 2017. The company’s products have a consistent presence in cleanups and brand audits completed around the world. In Break Free From Plastic’s 2020 annual report, Nestlé, along with Coca-Cola and PepsiCo, were named the world’s top plastic polluters for the third year in a row. (greenpeace.ch)

Fine: none
