Volkswagen

In April 2017, a US federal judge ordered Volkswagen to pay a criminal fine of $2.8 billion for cheating emissions tests and deceptive advertising. However, this classic greenwashing scandal has ended up costing them over 30 billion euros. They had fitted around 11 million of its diesel cars with ‘defeat devices’, designed to cheat emissions tests. Volkswagen admitted to cheating on the tests and the company's CEO resigned shortly after the scandal unraveled.

Fine: $2,800,000,000
