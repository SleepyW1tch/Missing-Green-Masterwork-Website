Problem Statement

Younger generations are exposed to vast amounts of information daily, yet they often lack the critical tools to discern truth from manipulation. Education plays a crucial role in shaping conscious consumers of online content. Sustainability and climate issues are particularly vulnerable to greenwashing and deceptive narratives.

Thesis: Misinformed World

Project Description

Pitch-Salad is a hybrid edutainment game for players aged 14+ that uses playful competition, physical and digital elements, and real-world examples to teach critical thinking about greenwashing and help recognize misleading environmental claims.
