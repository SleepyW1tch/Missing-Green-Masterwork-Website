Outcome: Ikea

Earthsight's report largely blamed the FSC for Ikea's alleged involvement in the scandal. Following the greenwashing accusations, Ikea said it parted ways with the suppliers. This case highlights the challenges of supply chain sustainability. Greenpeace found IKEA suppliers logging ancient forests in Romania, including protected areas—undermining IKEA’s sustainability claims. (Greenpeace 2024, disclose.ngo)

Penalty: none
