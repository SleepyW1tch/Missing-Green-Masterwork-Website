Outcome: KLM

An Amsterdam court ruled that KLM's advertising was misleading and mandated the airline to communicate honestly about its CO₂ reduction ambitions. ​The court said that in other cases, “KLM paints an overly rosy picture of the effects of measures such as Sustainable Aviation Fuels (made from renewable raw materials) and reforestation.” The court said such measures “only marginally reduce the negative environmental aspects and give the wrong impression that flying with KLM is sustainable.” (image: karryon.com.au)

Fine: none
