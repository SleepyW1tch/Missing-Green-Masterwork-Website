McDonald’s

McDonald’s is one of the largest beef purchasers in the world and the vast majority of its total emissions are produced by its supply chain, which includes beef and other. (ecowatch.com) McDonald's has opened its first net zero restaurant in 2021, but some experts have accused the fast food chain of "greenwashing". (news.sky.com) "If meat and dairy are still the main course on McDonald's menu, then this new restaurant initiative can only be labelled as it is: McGreenwash." (image: myshadeofgreen.com)
