Nike

Fashionista’s reporter describes it as a mere “marketing campaign that repackages old commitments without offering new ones”. Nike’s Chief Sustainability Officer admitted that some of the targets the company set itself were not entirely realistic and might actually be more difficult to reach than initially thought. They faced a class-action lawsuit in 2023 alleging that it misrepresented the sustainability of its "Sustainability Collection" products. In 2024, a federal judge in Missouri dismissed the lawsuit.
