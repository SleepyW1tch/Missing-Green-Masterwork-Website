Coca-Cola

A report is highlighting that the company alone produces 200,000 tonnes of plastic waste in countries like China, India, the Philippines, Brazil, Mexico and Nigeria which is equivalent to 8 billion plastic bottles. They would be trialing its new 100% recyclable paper-based bottles in Hungary which will have a paper shell made with a bio-based water-resistant barrier lining. It is working with a company to develop all-plant bottles that are biodegradable and recyclable. The damage the beverage giant causes far outweighs these proposals.
