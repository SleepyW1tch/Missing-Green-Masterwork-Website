Toyota

In 2021, Toyota was ordered to pay $180 million for violating the US Clean Air Act. It's worth noting that this is the largest penalty ever given to a company for violating the EPA’s emission-reporting requirements. A spokesperson for Toyota said they alerted the authorities when they discovered the defect. However, they added that "some of our reporting protocols fell short of our high standards,(...)”. A 2023 AAP report claimed Toyota could be facing a fine of $50 million in Australia for “misleading” plans to reach net-zero by 2050. (ozarab.media)
