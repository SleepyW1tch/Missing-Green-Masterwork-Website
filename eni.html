Eni

Eni stopped running the ads for its biodeisel. But its greenwashing turmoil didn’t end there. Italian regulators fined Eni €5 million ($5.6 million) after the oil company advertised its Eni Diesel+ fuel as ‘green.’ Eni then reportedly put plans in place to sue all the NGOs back, with a spokesperson saying: “We strongly believe, not only that this does not constitute greenwashing, but rather it is a serious way to face the energy trilemma: energy security, competitiveness, and decarbonization.” (ilfattoquotidiano.it)
