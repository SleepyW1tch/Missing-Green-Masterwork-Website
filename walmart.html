Outcome: Walmart

In 2022, US retailers Kohl’s and Walmart agreed to pay a $5.5 million collective settlement over suggestions that they’d used vague and misleading claims when marketing products as bamboo, when actually they were made from other materials. A spokesperson for Walmart also said: “We are committed to being the most trusted retailer and take these claims seriously. We hold ourselves accountable when issues like this are raised.” (ilsr.org)
