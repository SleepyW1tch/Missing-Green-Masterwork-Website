Outcome: DWS (Deutsche Bank)

In 2023, the Deutsche Bank-backed investment firm DWS agreed to a $25 million settlement over ESG misstatement allegations. DWS vowed to continue developing itself as an ESG leader following the fine.  They previously paid a $19 million fine to the U.S. SEC for similar issues. Although $25 million is a relatively small sum for large firms, investigations and legal cases make headlines, which can damage brands' reputations. (rdl.de)
