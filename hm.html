Outcome: H&M

H&M donated €400,000 to sustainable causes in 2022 as compensation for making unclear sustainability claims. A spokesperson for H&M also said the company’s labeling “could have been clearer and more comprehensive.”  H&M reportedly removed sustainable labeling from their stores and websites following the investigation to avoid being fined. Misleading marketing has worn down customer trust in corporate sustainability initiatives. Since the payout, H&M has faced a lawsuit in 2023 over misleading sustainability labels. (just-style.com)
