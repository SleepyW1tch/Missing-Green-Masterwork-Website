Starbucks

In 2018, Starbucks released a “straw-less lid,” as part of its sustainability drive, however this lid contained more plastic than the old lid and straw combination. The company didn’t dispute this. Starbucks claimed its coffee cups were recyclable, but due to the plastic lining, they could only be recycled in a few specialized facilities. They are facing a lawsuit filed in 2024 by the National Consumers League. The lawsuit accuses Starbucks of misleading customers with claims that its coffee and tea are "100% ethically sourced," despite alleged human rights abuses on supplier farms. (nclnet.org, breakfreefrom plastic.org)

Penalty: none, lawsuit ongoing
