Outcome: Keurig

Canadian regulators ordered them to pay $2.2 million after making misleading claims to customers about its single-use coffee pods. As part of the settlement, Keurig agreed to donate $593,000 to a Canadian charity committed to environmental efforts. A larger payout followed in the US. In 2023, Keurig settled a $10 million class action suit in the US, where plaintiffs claimed the company misled its consumers. Their packaging was later updated to reflect recycling nuances, saying the pods are "not recycled in all communities."
