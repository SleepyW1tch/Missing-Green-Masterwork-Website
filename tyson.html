Tyson Foods

The Environmental Working Group (EWG) filed a lawsuit in 2024, alleging that Tyson's statements were misleading and lacked substantiation. In 2025, the D.C. Superior Court denied Tyson's motion to dismiss the case, allowing the lawsuit to proceed. As of now, no penalties have been imposed, and the case remains ongoing.
